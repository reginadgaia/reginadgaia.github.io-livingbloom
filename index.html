From Global Systems to Personal Renewal

Hello, I’m Mara Regina D’Gaia.

For more than 25 years, I worked in the complex world of climate policy and global systems. Over time, I came to understand something deeply personal: when we live under constant pressure, without space to listen to ourselves, something essential begins to wear down. I also came to see that the wider changes I had spent years supporting through engineering and policy can only take root in a lasting way when people themselves are able to reconnect, rebalance, and change from within.

Since 2009, I have been on a deep path of inner work and personal renewal. Every tool I propose is something I have personally used, studied, and integrated in my own life.

Today, I offer a grounded and supportive space for people who need to slow down, reconnect with themselves, and move through change with more clarity, steadiness, and care.
